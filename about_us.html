About Us

Our History

Booking.com was founded in Amsterdam, Netherlands, in 1996, and was initially known as Booking.nl — a platform focused exclusively on hotel reservations within the Netherlands. Over the following decades, the platform expanded rapidly to become one of the world's largest online travel agencies, offering accommodation in more than 220 countries and territories.

Today, Booking.com is part of Booking Holdings Inc. and connects millions of travellers with over 28 million accommodation listings worldwide, ranging from budget hostels and guesthouses to five-star luxury resorts and private holiday homes.

Our Mission

To make it easier for everyone to experience the world by providing a trusted, easy-to-use platform where travellers can find and book the accommodation and travel services that best suit their needs.

Our Vision

To afford every traveller the opportunity to use technology to conveniently discover and book hotels, apartments and experiences across the globe — at the best possible price.
